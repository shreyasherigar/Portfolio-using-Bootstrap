Smart Med Box

2022-Present

The medical box comes coupled with a phone application that facilitates the initial setup and remote operation of the medical box. Users can use the app to enter a list of pills and then assign them to different slots in the schedule in varying quantities. The app then transfers the schedule to an Arduino board in the box, which then controls the locking mechanisms and the dispenser systems to facilitate the operation of the box.
